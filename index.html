A little about me,

in 2021 I decided to make the switch from working as a Chef de Partie, to focus on my other passion, IT. My first memory with computers has to be JazzJack Rabbit 2 In the early 2000s. If you don't know, it's a side-scrolling shooter kinda game, with all sorts of weird hallucinogenic themes. Great stuff. Then, I went on to play Diablo II for almost a decade and the World of Warcraft, oh how, far the mighty have fallen. Aside from games, I love all things tech. I built my first PC when I was 12 and ever since then, I've been the "nerd-to-call" within my social circles. Aside from games, I'm into all things tech. Currently I'm learning CSS (fuck you!), HTML and C#, and Linux. However, I have a solid feeling I won't be able to escape JavaScript (hopefully TypeScript?).

We'll see.

What can you expect?

Changes. Lots of changes. It's going to be my little sandbox, where I can try out and testdrive new ideas or things that I've learned. One week that might be CSS, the other it might be JS or C# (perhaps Rust?) It will also be used to showcase different projects I've taken part in. So kind of like a portfolio. It's neat to little page I can document my work with.

If you don't know, it's a side-scrolling shooter kinda game, with all sorts of weird hallucinogenic themes. Great stuff. Then, I went on to play Diablo II for almost a decade and the World of Warcraft, oh how, far the mighty have fallen. Aside from games, I love all things tech. I built my first PC when I was 12 and ever since then, I've been the "nerd-to-call" within my social circles. Aside from games, I'm into all things tech.
